Banana Chocolate-Chip Muffins

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tspn vanilla
  • 1 tspn baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Grease your muffin tins
  3. Peel bananas and throw them in a big bowl with the melted butter and smash them all together
  4. Keep blending until the mixture is creamy and smooth
  5. Add sugar, egg and vanilla and mix unitl combined
  6. Stir in salt and baking soda
  7. Enjoy!
